Hossein Hajimirsadeghi

Senior Machine Learning Researcher at Borealis AI


I received my PhD in 2015 under supervision of Prof. Greg Mori. Currently, I am a Senior Machine Learning Researcher at Borealis AI, Vancouver. Prior to that I was a Principal Member of Technical Staff at Oracle Labs, Vancouver.

Ph.D. in Computer Science, Simon Fraser University, Canada, 2015.
